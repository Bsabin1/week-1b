 This movie is and incredible found footage film. And is set very close to here for a horror movie. this movie was created by young adults as a project and it became one of the most cult followed movies in the horror franchise. It follows 3 friends that go looking for this entity called the Blair Witch upon entering the woods the whole party feels the tension as the story line builds to the inevitable hauntings that come in the movie.

 After the first slight haunting the group loses it and tries to find there friend who is now gone. In doing so the other friends go deeper into the woods which becomes increasingly clear that this was a trap to lure them closer. the reason that this movie became such a cult classic is that the monster isnt ever shown. This lets the views imagination run wild and create whatever there fear is in the monster.
